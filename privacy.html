Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Unsaid respects privacy, especially in a space built for what cannot be spoken aloud.

1. Information We Collect

Unsaid does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to post content.

However, limited data may be collected automatically, including IP addresses, basic server logs, browser information, device data, and cookies or similar technologies used by hosting, analytics, or advertising services.

2. Anonymous Content

Posts submitted to Unsaid are anonymous by design. Users are responsible for ensuring they do not include identifying details about themselves or others.

Absolute anonymity cannot be guaranteed, particularly if disclosure is required by law.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Services

Unsaid may rely on third-party services such as hosting providers, analytics tools, or advertising networks. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. How Information Is Used

Information collected may be used to maintain and secure the website, improve performance, and comply with legal obligations. Unsaid does not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary for operational or legal purposes.

6. Legal Disclosure

Information may be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the platform.

7. Children’s Privacy

Unsaid is not intended for users under the age of 13. Content from minors should not be submitted.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
